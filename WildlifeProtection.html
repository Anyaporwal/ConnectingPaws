Wildlife Protection Act, 1972

Key Provisions
Section 9: Prohibits hunting of any wild animal specified in Schedules I, II, III and IV
Section 39: All animals in Schedule I and Part II of Schedule II are government property
Section 48A: Prohibits transportation of wildlife without permission
Section 51: Penalties for violations (see below)

Penalties

Hunting Schedule I animals: 3-7 years imprisonment + ₹10 lakh fine
Illegal trade in wildlife: Up to ₹25 lakh fine + 7 years jail
Hunting Schedule II animals: Up to 3 years jail + ₹25,000 fine
Damage to habitat: 3-7 years jail + ₹25,000 fine
Protected Areas
National Parks (Section 35): No human activity allowed except permitted by Chief Wildlife Warden
Wildlife Sanctuaries (Section 26A): Limited human activities permitted
